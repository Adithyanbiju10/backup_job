About Us

AbilityJobs is dedicated to bridging the gap between talented individuals with disabilities and inclusive employers. We believe that everyone deserves equal opportunities in the workforce.

Our platform uses smart technology to adapt to your specific needs, whether you require voice assistance, screen reader compatibility, or wheelchair-accessible job listings.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in your details, and verify your email address.

Is the voice search available on mobile?

Yes! Our voice search works on both desktop and mobile browsers. Use Chrome on Android or Safari on iOS for best results.

How do employers post jobs?

Employers can register for a free account, then click "Post a Job" from their dashboard to create and manage job listings.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never share your information without your consent.
